The Clockmaker's Daughter

In the perpetually twilight Town of Clocks, time is frozen, guarded only by Amara, the clockmaker's lonely daughter. She is the prisoner of the Great Clock, destined for eternal solitude—a fate disrupted when Elara, a traveler trying to outrun her own past, arrives. Their impossible love unleashes a devastating storm of temporal chaos, threatening to shatter the fragile mechanisms of existence. Amara and Elara are forced to make a final choice: separation, or an eternal bond that risks plunging their world into the swirling, silver chaos of a time rift.

Salt In Her Veins

On a fog-drenched island where memory clings like salt to skin, a woman inherits more than a crumbling house-she inherits silence, stories, and the slow pull of something she thought she'd forgotten. As the sea whispers and the past resurfaces, two women find themselves drawn together by what the island remembers, and what it refuses to let go. Not everything buried stays quiet. Not every love story begins with light.
